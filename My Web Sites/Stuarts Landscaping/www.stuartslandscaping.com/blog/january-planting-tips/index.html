January Planting Tips

  • Posted on January 12, 2014

1st Week of January

  • Christmas Lights
    • Take down Christmas Lights and look for dead branches.

2nd Week of January

  • Planning
    • Spring will be here before you know it!! During the rest of the month start planning for your summer gardens or landscaping needs.
    • Think back to what you liked and disliked about last years gardens.
    • Look through plant catalogs and start taking notes or give Stuart's a call and we would love you help you out!!
