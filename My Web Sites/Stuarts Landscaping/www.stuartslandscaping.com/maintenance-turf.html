Maintenance & Turf

"I would like a low maintenance landscape..."

That is what 95% of our clients of our Garden Center or Design clients say.

The truth is that landscapes, like any other investment -- your home, car, boat -- need to be maintained. Fortunately, unlike a car or boat, a well maintained landscape increases in value.

Stuart's Landscaping provides a wide range of maintenance services, each tailored to meet the specific needs of the homeowners and property managers. These services include:

  • Spring and Fall Clean Ups
  • Annual Flower Planting ( Beds or Planters)
  • Weekly Turf Mowing and Trimming
  • Weekly, Bi-Weekly, or Monthly Horticultural Maintenance Visits :
    • Weeding of Planting Beds
    • Fertilization of Trees/ Shrubs / Perennials
    • Pruning of Shrubs and Perennials
    • Debris Removal
  • Winter Protection of Trees/ Shrubs to minimize rodent damage
  • Snow Plowing, Salting
  • Lawn Thatching & Aeration
  • Lawn Fertilization & Weed Management
  • Tree Injections
  • Irrigation Maintenance
