Garden Centers

Stuart's Garden Centers have a valued reputation of providing the Fond du Lac, Oshkosh, Ripon, and the surrounding Fox Valley with the highest quality, well maintained nursery stock. With the help of the previous purchase of "Gro-Plant" in 2006, Stuart's now offers high quality plant material to Oshkosh, Neenah, Menasha, and all surrounding areas in the Fox Valley. Stuart's staff of trained horticulturists, pride themselves on providing a wide selection of award winning trees and shrubs, garden art, and landscaping materials. Some of the Garden Center highlights are:

  • Large, Locally Grown Trees, Shade, Ornamentals, and Evergreens
  • Huge Selection of Perennials, and Ornamental Grasses
  • "Proven Winners" Annual Bedding Plants, Hanging Baskets.
  • "Bud & Bloom" and "Easy Elegance" Roses. Other roses to delight you are Forty Heroes, Perfume Delight, and Hot Cocoa just to name a few.
  • Pre-Planted Container Gardens
  • Unique Statuary, Memorial Benches, Gazing Globes, Stoneware.
  • Ornamental Conifer Garden
  • Wide Selection of Wood Mulches and Decorative Stones.
  • Chemicals such as: Fertilizers and Soil Amendments, Repellents, Insect Control, Fungicide and Weed Control. Stuart's now offers the same line in Natural and Organic Products.
  • Visit Stuart's today and let our trained and knowledgeable staff assist you in turning your gardening inspiration into a living work of art.
