Patios, Pergolas and Fire Pits

Patios are quickly becoming one of the most used "rooms" in a home. Patios serve as great gathering and entertaining spaces, where your friends and family can enjoy each other, while enjoying the outdoors.

Today many patios are constructed with innovative features like seating walls, built in cooking stations and landscape planters. All these features are possible due to the advances in paving and wall systems, which make constructing multi-level, multi- use spaces easy and long lasting.
